# Decision Record Template

ADR-style template for documenting important design decisions.

**Front matter example:**

```yaml
---
title: [Decision Title]
date: YYYY-MM-DD
owner: @username
status: proposed | accepted | rejected | superseded
tags: [decision, adr, tag3]
---
```
